We recommend that you visit our websites periodically to be aware of and review any such revisions. We may also notify you by other means, such as posting notices on our websites or sending notifications to you. As a user, you will have the chance to accept or reject the updated Privacy Policy.

 

What information about you we may collect

 

We may obtain your Personal Information from various sources:

a. Personal Information directly from you

When you install our apps, you’ll be asked to provide some basic information about you for registration purposes. As a result of the use of our apps, we may collect Personal Information that you provide voluntarily such as videos recorded by Ryusaki-enabled camera products. This may include capturing and emailing to you portions of the videos as part of a notification or analyzing the data to identify motion or other events. For the purpose of customer support, we may collect information through your communications with us.

 

b. Personal Information from your Interactions with our apps and services

We use automatic data collection tools like cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies in our products and services to collect service-enabling data, including IP addresses, device identifiers and other information about your device, system and application software, and peripherals. We collect information related to the use our apps and statistics about the performance of your Ryusaki--enabled devices. This information includes internet speed, voltage information, storage information, and error rates.

 

c. Personal Information from a third-party

It is possible for cameras owned and operated by you to capture photos or videos of a third party.

How we protect your Personal Information

 

We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your Personal Information against accidental or unlawful destruction, accidental loss, unauthorized alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access, misuse, and any other unlawful form of processing of the Personal Information in our possession. We cannot promise that your use of our apps and services will be completely safe.

 

We take measures to delete your Personal Information or keep your identity anonymous when the information is no longer necessary for the purposes for which we process it or when you request its deletion, unless we are allowed or required by law to keep the information for a longer period. We conduct periodic reviews of our databases, and have established time limits for certain data deletion, taking into consideration the type of services provided in the context of the app products and services, the length of our relationship with you, mandatory retention periods, and the statute of limitations.

Only individuals 18 years of age or older are permitted to act as owners of Ryusaki accounts. Our app products and services are meant for adults. We do not knowingly allow children under 18 years of age to set up accounts. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has disclosed Personal Information to us, please contact us. We will take such notices seriously and address the issue properly.
